The Two's

Most 2-year-olds are filled with energy and an innate curiosity about the world around them. They’re also quickly developing their sense of independence. We offer opportunities for them to explore this growing individuality. Stimulating, engaging environment, exclusive milestone-based Christian curriculum, immersive learning experiences, variety of tactile learning materials. This active classroom environment engages your child’s body, hands, minds and imaginations. Learning experiences in this classroom address the evolving developmental needs of this mobile age group, while milestone development continues to be guided by our Christian curriculum. The ability to solve problems, explore the world around them, become more independent. The ability to coordinate and control large and small muscle movements; increasing awareness of health and safety concepts. The ability to communicate effectively using sounds, expressions, body language, and finally, oral and written language; ability to understand and respond to the communication of others are all things we reinforce throughout their time in the 2’s classroom. We will also begin potty training in this class. We have a child sized potty in the class, and amazing potty-training teachers. While they need to be trained before they can move up to our older classrooms, we understand that every child is ready at different times. When you as a parent are ready, we will help with the potty-training journey ahead.
