Playschool

The Playschool room is for kids ages 2 1/2 to 3 1/2 years old, and are potty trained. Each child learns in different ways. The CDC Christian curriculum provides teachers with the tools and guidance to accommodate childrenâ€™s natural curiosity and to fully support each child while building problem solving skills and confidence. In this classroom, children learn to work with others, follow directions, self-regulate, follow routines, and develop self help skills. Engaging signs show picture schedules to help with understanding routines. This classroom is instilling a sense of independence amongst the children. They also continue to learn and enhance their understanding of colors, shapes, counting and learning the alphabet, but they are also introduced to cutting with scissors, using glue, holding markers and crayons the correct way and discovering new textures with art supplies. We also encourge dressing themselves, using words instead of pointing, and lining up.
