The Pewee Valley Presbyterian Church Child Development Center is committed to quality child care and preschool education in a Christian setting.

The Child Development Center is a State of Kentucky Licensed Facility and a participant in the Kentucky STARS program.

The Child Development Center at Pewee Valley Presbyterian provides:

  • An award-winning state-licensed childcare and preschool program for children ages 6 weeks through Pre-Kindergarten, (7 a.m. – 6 p.m. Monday through Friday).
  • A safe, warm environment where self-esteem is developed in preparing children for Kindergarten and other life experiences.
  • A Christ-centered environment designed to meet the social, intellectual, emotional, physical and spiritual needs of your child.
  • Parent education fellowship opportunities.
  • Summer programs for school age children, preschoolers and enrichment camps.
