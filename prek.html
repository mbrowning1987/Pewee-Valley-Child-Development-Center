Pre-K

This class is for our 4 year olds, some of them will be graduating, and the others at the end of the year will move over to our Jr-K room. In this class, they learn their ABC's and numbers. They also learn how to reconize them, and write them. They begin to learn the letters in their first and last names, and writing it. They will continue with the days of the week, months of the year and seasons. They have more freedom over the art center, and new manipulative's for the kids to play with. They work on how to hold pencils/scissors and utensil's correctly. Our center uses Handwriting Without Tears handbook to teach these concepts to the children.
