Tuition

Weekly tuition payments (by check) are due on Monday's for the current week. Payment's are considered late at the close of business on Tuesday and a $15 late fee will be assesed. Sorry, NO exceptions can be made. Please make check or money order payable to the CDC and place your payment in the lock box by the office. Do not place payment's in back packs, diaper bags, or hand them to teachers. Returned checks will be charged a $20.00 penalty. In regard to holidays, illness or closure of the CDC due to severe weather, we do not make adjustments or give creit. The CDC board of Directors will assess potential adjustments or credits for tuition if the CDC closes, due to unusual circumstances, on an individual basis. Children will be dismissed for delinquent fees for non-payment.

Vacations/Holidays

The CDC will close one week during the Christmas holiday. Children enrolled at the Child Development Center will not pay tuition for the week the center is closed. This is the only tuition free week. The CDC also observes seven major holidays each year and tuition rates remain in effect as our full time employees are paid for those holidays.

Late Pick-ups

The CDC closes at 6:00pm. There is a late pick up fee of $5.00 per child for pick up between 6:00pm and 6:10pm. An addional charge of $1.00 per minute will be assessed until your child is picked up from the center. Late pick up fees must be paid upon your arrival. Licensing allows the CDC to operate from 7:00am to 6:00pm, Monday through Friday. Social services will be called for any child who os not picked up by 7:00pm. The director's phone is the offical CDC time. Continual late pick ups will result in dismissal of your child from the center.

Financial Assistance

The CDC participates in the Kentucky Funded Child Care Assistance Program: CCAP/3-C's. In addition, a small amount of money may be available for financial assistance to families that have experienced a temporary financial crisis due to medical issues, loss of job, etc. Please inquire with the director for more information.
