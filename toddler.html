Toddler's

From toys, books, computers, wall mirror imagery, and above all, caring, loving, daily role models. Your child will experience a day of individual and group play, circle times with stories, songs and finger plays, appropriate exposure to art and music and lots of special individual attention to nurture what ever is interesting to her that day. This classroom is for children 1 year old to 2 years old. They have transitioned to one nap a day, no bottles during the day and fully on solid food.
