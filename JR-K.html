JR-K Class

This classroom is structured pretty much like a Kindergarten classroom, the kids are 5 or almost 5 missing the cutoff to Kindergarten, except with more play. The kid's continue to learn their ABC's in upper and lowercase. They continue to work on their numbers, and their names. When those skills are mastered, the class begins to count by 10's and 5's, learning sight words, basic math (simple addition and subtraction), months of the year, days of the week. They continue to work on basic motor skills, like holding pencils correctly, cutting with scissors, eating with utensils the correct way. The kid's also have freeplay everyday, and a pretty awesome art center. Check out the classroom scedule below for more details.
