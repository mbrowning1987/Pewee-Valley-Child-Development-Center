Transition's

This classroom is for our babies, that have just started sitting up, unsupported, or crawling. They are eating some table food. We have a lot to offer your little explorer. Now that they are becoming more mobile, there are new activities and learning opportunities awaiting them. We have buggie's to take them outside in, art becomes something they do often. It may be messy, but they are learning new textures. They transition from several naps a day, to one over time. Thye learn to sleep on cots, and to sit at a table.
