Beginnergarten

This classroom is for our older 3's to young 4's. While they continue to learn independence and self help skills, we also continue to enhance their knowledge of colors, shapes, numbers and letters. They are introduced to a new letter and number weekly. They are also introduced to the days of the week, months of the year, and begin to start recognizing their first names. While this is not a Pre-K classroom, they are beginning to learn the concepts of a Pre-K classroom. They continue to do art everyday, circle time and special activities that go along with our Christian curriculum.
